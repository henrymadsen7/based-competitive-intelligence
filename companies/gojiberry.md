# Gojiberry.ai — Competitive Intelligence
## Date: 2026-04-20

### Core Value Prop
"Find & Contact High-Intent Leads With AI." AI detects intent signals, scores prospects based on ideal customers, starts relevant conversations on LinkedIn, books more demos on autopilot.

### Key Features
- Find warm leads on autopilot (10+ intent signals)
- Only ICP-matched leads pre-filtered
- AI outreach that books demos (smart personalized LinkedIn messages)
- Track signals & campaigns that convert
- 30+ intent signals
- 2 LinkedIn senders (Pro plan)
- Unlimited LinkedIn campaigns
- Unlimited warm leads
- AI-powered outreach with smart lead scoring
- Email Waterfall Enrichment (+15 data providers)
- CRM & API integrations (HubSpot, Pipedrive)
- Chat & email support

### Pricing
- Pro: $99/mo — 30+ intent signals, 2 LinkedIn senders, unlimited campaigns, unlimited warm leads, AI outreach, email waterfall enrichment, CRM/API integrations
- Custom: Talk with us — for small sales teams (5+), more LinkedIn accounts, more intent signals, dedicated CSM, deep custom integrations

### Target Audience
B2B founders, SDRs, solo operators, small sales teams.

### Tech Stack Signals
- LinkedIn-focused
- 15 data providers for enrichment
- CRM integrations

### UX/UI Patterns
- Simple 3-step onboarding (sign up, pick signals, launch outreach)
- Campaign reply rate analytics
- Clean, simple landing page

### Integration Ecosystem
HubSpot, Pipedrive, Slack, LinkedIn, 15 data providers.

### Competitive Weaknesses/Gaps
- LinkedIn-only channel (no email, no phone, no other social)
- No multi-channel outreach sequences
- No visual workflow builder
- No team collaboration features beyond CRM sync
- Pricing is steep for solo operators ($99/mo entry)

### Reverse-Engineering Notes
Gojiberry validates AI outbound sales as a category.
To beat/absorb them:
- Multi-channel outreach (LinkedIn, email, X, phone, SMS)
- Visual campaign builder
- Intent signals from ANY source (not just LinkedIn)
- Team collaboration on outreach
- Lower entry price or freemium model
